# FOSSIA Spotlights

FOSSIA Spotlights is an initiative by [FOSSIA](https://fossia.org/) to celebrate and acknowledge first-time contributors and mentors to our free and open-source projects.

# Contributing

Made a contribution to our projects and you're a first-timer in free and open-source ecosystem? Congratulations, we are so happy for you!

Mentoring our projects for enabling newcomers to make their first contributions? Hats off!

[Check out our contributing guide for adding your experience as a blog to get featured for your efforts.](/CONTRIBUTING.md)
